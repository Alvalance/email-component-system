Origin Story
They call it Big History: an attempt to stitch all of Earth's past into one global story. Australian professor David Christian has received plaudits (and major funding) from Bill Gates, and his new book Origin Story takes us from the Big Bang to the evolution of life on earth and—only recently—human society. The ambitious but flawed work turns out to be a case against parochial perspectives on human existence and for coordinated global action, writes reviewer David Wootton. But shouldn’t his study of history have made him realize how unlikely such cooperation will be?
